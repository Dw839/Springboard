# Customer Acquisition Funnel Analysis: Final Capstone Project

## 📋 Executive Summary

This capstone project analyzes customer acquisition performance by examining lead behavior throughout the sales funnel. The objective was to identify factors influencing customer engagement, conversion success, and lead attrition while providing actionable recommendations to improve acquisition effectiveness.

Using multiple datasets containing lead demographics, customer interactions, demo engagement activity, lead assignment information, and conversion outcomes, a comprehensive analytical framework was developed to evaluate the customer journey from initial contact to conversion.

The project demonstrates the application of data analytics, business intelligence, and process improvement methodologies to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations invest significant resources in attracting and nurturing prospective customers. However, many leads fail to progress through the sales funnel, resulting in lower conversion rates and missed revenue opportunities.

Key business questions addressed in this project include:

* What characteristics define the most engaged leads?
* Where do leads drop off in the acquisition process?
* What factors influence conversion success?
* What are the primary reasons for non-conversion?
* How can the customer acquisition process be improved?

---

## 📊 Project Objectives

The primary objectives were to:

* Assess data quality and prepare datasets for analysis.

* Analyze customer demographics and engagement behavior.

## 📊 Key Findings

### Customer Demographics

* The majority of leads were between **18 and 24 years old**.
* Most leads were pursuing higher education or actively seeking employment opportunities.

### Lead Generation Performance

* Social media was a major source of customer acquisition.
* SEO and email marketing also contributed significantly to lead generation efforts.

### Customer Engagement

* English was the most preferred demo language.
* Leads who engaged more deeply with demo content were more likely to progress through the funnel.

### Conversion Analysis

* Total Leads Evaluated: **354**
* Successfully Converted Leads: **64**
* Overall Conversion Rate: **18.08%**

### Lead Attrition Drivers

The primary reasons for non-conversion included:

* Affordability concerns
* Preference for offline learning options
* Lack of interest in the subject area
* Time constraints

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following opportunities were identified:

### Improve Early Funnel Engagement

Strengthen lead nurturing efforts during the awareness and consideration stages.

### Increase Demo Participation

Encourage higher demo attendance and completion rates through targeted follow-ups.

### Address Affordability Concerns

Develop flexible pricing options and alternative payment solutions.

### Prioritize High-Intent Leads

Focus sales resources on prospects demonstrating stronger engagement signals.

### Optimize Follow-Up Strategy

Improve communication cadence and personalization throughout the customer journey.
